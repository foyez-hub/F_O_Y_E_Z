EXTRA! EXTRA! WIZARDRY UNFOLDS AT UIU!

In a spellbinding chapter of magical academia, Emam Hasan, the aspiring wizard hailing from United International University (UIU), has etched his name in the sacred scrolls of knowledge. The four-year journey from 2020 to 2024 has proven to be a captivating tale of academic pursuit and mystical enlightenment. Emam's wand, wielded not of wood but of code, has conjured remarkable feats within the realm of Computer Science and Engineering. His academic excellence, reflected in a CGPA of 3.61, is a testament to the wizardry imparted by UIU's esteemed professors.

Merit has not eluded this budding sorcerer, as he has been bestowed with magical scholarships – a 50% enchantment twice and a 25% charm four times. Such honors are a recognition of Emam's dedication to the art of knowledge. The enchanted curriculum at UIU's School of Sorcery has unfolded a myriad of subjects before Emam. From the intricacies of Data Structures and Algorithms Analysis to the artistry of Software Engineering, the mystical realms of Database Management, and the secrets concealed within Artificial Intelligence, his academic quest has been nothing short of extraordinary.

Cinephiles and Library Enchantment System: Muggle-Friendly Techno-Magic Unveiled!

Cinephiles: Step into the mystical world of cinema with Emam Hasan's latest creation, "Cinephiles' Haven." This bewitching muggle-friendly web app, crafted with the powers of Laravel, PHP, JavaScript, Ajax, MySQL, HTML, and CSS, invites movie enthusiasts to explore, share lists, attend watch parties, and join a vibrant community. Led by Emam's magical touch, the project features an innovative meme contest, casting spells of creativity, and robust privacy charms for user profiles, ensuring a personalized and secure cinematic experience

Library Enchantment System: Emam Hasan, the technological wizard, has harnessed the powers of IoT to create a "Library Enchantment System." Through the wizardry of JavaScript, Ajax, Arduino Uno, Node Mcu, Firebase, HTML, and CSS, this muggle-accessible cloud-based marvel not only manages libraries seamlessly but also incorporates Sound Sensors for real-time monitoring of the library's magical ambiance. The result is a spellbinding interface for all bookworms and knowledge seekers.

Muggle Attempts To Rob Gringotts

It’s not uncommon in the muggle world to hear about bank robberies – but it is unusual that you would hear about Muggles trying to rob Wizarding Banks.

For the first time in history, a muggle-man was reported to have marched into Gringotts Bank in Diagon Alley at approximately 11 am waving around a black wand that was described to project pieces of elemental lead, also known to Muggles as a gun.

While no one was hurt during the transgression, the question looms – how exactly did a Muggle get into Diagon Alley and furthermore, what would a Muggle need with Wizarding Currency?

When we caught up with one of the Goblins who was at the bank during the time of the incident, he let us know “The man who came into Gringotts did not speak, not even once. He walked in, went straight to the first clerk that was available and passed them a howler. The howler began screaming in a dark ominous voice that if Gringotts did not hand over all Golden Galleons in the bank’s possession, we would all be subject to death. You just can't trust those muggle folk, none of them.”
